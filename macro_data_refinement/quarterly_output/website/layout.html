Facility Layout

This chart documents room assignments across the three active facility levels. Rooms are organized by level and aisle designation; aisle codes correspond to the bin classifications used throughout the processing framework. New refiners are encouraged to locate their assigned aisle and familiarize themselves with adjacent support services. Calibration facilities are noted by name where present on each level; refiners who have been directed toward calibration by their supervisor should consult this chart to orient themselves before proceeding. For a full reference of bin codes and department assignments, see the Processing Manual and Department Directory.

Room assignments are maintained by Facilities Administration. Calibration schedules are administered separately by the Office of Employee Recognition and are not reflected in this chart. For access queries, contact your designated supervisor.
